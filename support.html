Frequently Asked Questions

  • Lap not detected? — Ensure GPS is enabled and you're outdoors with a clear sky view.
  • Heart rate not showing? — Make sure Apple Watch is worn and HealthKit access is granted in Settings.
  • Sessions not syncing? — Keep iPhone and Watch nearby with Bluetooth enabled.
  • Trial expired? — The app locks after the 30-day trial. Unlock the full version to continue using lap timing and restore leaderboard visibility.
  • Leaderboard lap missing? — Trial laps are visible during the trial. After expiry, leaderboard entries may be hidden until the full version is unlocked.
  • App crashes on launch? — Try restarting the app and ensure your iOS is up to date.
  • How to delete my account? — Go to Profile tab → scroll down → tap "Delete Account".

Privacy & Data

AKSYS LapTimer uses GPS location, motion sensors, and Apple HealthKit heart rate when permission is granted. Session and leaderboard data is stored in Firebase and is never sold to third parties.
