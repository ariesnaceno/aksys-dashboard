NEW
MOUNT CALIBRATION
One-tap gravity calibration for the exact Quad Lock mount angle. Removes offset for accurate G-Force and lean.
REAL G-FORCE
CMDeviceMotion separates gravity from user acceleration. Shows true braking/cornering G, not raw accelerometer noise.

Gravity vector lean calculation. Shows left/right lean degrees in real time. Calibrated for portrait Quad Lock mount.
